Hello, this is Lizhuang Tan.

I'm a .

In June 2022, I received my Ph.D. degree from the National Engineering Research Center of Advanced Network Technologies of Beijing Jiaotong University.

Since July 2022, I have been working in the Computing-Network Integration Research Group of Shandong Computer Science Center (National Supercomputer Center in Ji'nan).

My research interest is the management and optimization of large-scale networks, especially Data Center Networks and Software-defined Networks.
